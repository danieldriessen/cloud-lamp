How to add it
  1. Open Settings → Apps → Safari and turn off “Not Secure Connection Warning”.
  2. In Safari, open this lamp’s address (type it with http:// — do not search for it): http://cloud-lamp.local/
  3. Tap Share → Add to Home Screen → Add. The lamp then opens like an app.
